About Bali

Bali is a tropical paradise located in Indonesia, known for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches, and coral reefs. The island is home to religious sites like the cliffside Uluwatu Temple.

Bali offers something for every traveler:

  • Stunning beaches with world-class surfing
  • Ancient temples and rich cultural heritage
  • Lush jungles and breathtaking waterfalls
  • Vibrant nightlife and delicious cuisine

Top Attractions

Uluwatu Temple

Perched on a cliff 70 meters above the sea, this ancient temple offers spectacular sunset views and traditional Kecak dance performances.

Tegallalang Rice Terraces

These iconic rice paddies feature beautifully sculpted, stepped rice fields that follow the traditional Balinese cooperative irrigation system.

Mount Batur

An active volcano that offers one of Bali's most popular sunrise treks with breathtaking views of the surrounding caldera and lake.
